About Me

I am currently a Software Engineering Fellow at Headstarter AI, where I am engaged in a comprehensive program aimed at deepening my skills in artificial intelligence and software engineering. This role enables me to build meaningful connections and stay at the forefront of technological advancements. I am passionate about embracing new challenges and participating in diverse communities that support both my personal and professional growth as a woman in tech. As someone who values mentorship and guidance, I enjoy supporting others in their journeys.

Outside of my academic and coding pursuits, I have a deep passion for the arts.
